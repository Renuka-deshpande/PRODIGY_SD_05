More About Clocks

Wall Clocks are one of those essential items that can add a remarkable element to any home decor. A fancy wall clock is one of the best ways to highlight any plain and dull wall. But finding a wall clock design that goes perfectly with the interiors is definitely a hard catch.

We present you with the best wall clock designs online on Meesho that can make your home look more aesthetic. You may not think it would be worth your while to add a decorative wall clock to your house. But trust us, they can change the entire look of the room instantly. A beautiful decorative wall clock will add a sense of grandeur and charm to any decor theme. You can choose from vintage clocks, pendulum clocks, wooden clocks, contemporary clocks and many more designs as per your taste.

Liven Up Your Walls With Decorative And Useful Clocks

At times, you may have noticed that certain walls in your room look a little bland and need to be spruced up. The question is, what do you do to add some interest to these walls? There are several ways to do this. You can hang a painting, put up a poster or a wall hanging, etc. While all of these are good options, there is one more item you can use to add some visual interest. A wall clock will not only serve as a decorative accent but also be functional for you. You can also add a table clock on your study table or bedside table. From vintage to artsy to contemporary, there are multiple thees and options that you can choose. And all of them are available at budget-friendly prices.

Types Of Wall Clocks And Alarm Clocks That You Can Get Online

You will find a huge variety of decorative clocks these days. The most popular ones are antique clocks, wooden clocks, abstract designs, and customized ones. You simply need to choose something that goes with the theme of the room and appeals to you personally. You can make your clock the statement piece or simply incorporate it into the existing decor. The key is to zero in on one that perfectly matches your personality and the vibe of your home. Then cleverly incorporate it into the look of the room so that it packs an aesthetic punch and looks cool too. Whether it adds a burst of colour, a dash of whimsy, or grounds the room with a stylish focal point, a wall clock is a must-have for the modern living room wall.

Antique Wall Clocks

If you are someone who is into history and loves the old world charm, these clocks are perfect for you. The beauty of this design lies in the vintage finish that is given to it. These clocks usually have the numbers displayed in Roman numerals, just like old fashioned clocks. They are usually made of wood or metal and have an analogue display type. Hang a big vintage wall clock right at the centre of your living room wall to make it the focal point of your room.

Digital Wall Clocks

This is definitely one of the most trendy and beautiful wall clocks that you would hang in your room. Because of the digital display, these clocks are easy to read. Unlike analogue clocks which will only show the time, digital clocks can have many more features like a calendar or temperature function. Get one in a bright colour or a printed pattern to add a pop of colour to the corner where you place it. This can be a nice gift for your friends and family too.

Designer Table Clocks

These days, clocks serve the purpose of home decor more than anything else. You can use any other device to tell the time, a watch, your phone, your laptop, etc. But a stylish designer table clock adds that touch of elegance to your study corner. Desk clocks come in so many fancy designs these days. Some have a pen stand attached to it, others come with idols and figurines or even business cardholders. You can get something that personally appeals to you.

LED Digital Alarm Clocks

An alarm clock that comes with an LED display is perfect for all the night owls who need to check the time even when the lights are out. Anyone can use this clock easily as it is very simple to operate. The time is usually displayed clearly on the screen and you can even adjust the brightness level of the screen easily. Get one that has an alarm function with a snooze button. If you want to invest a little more you can get the latest ones which can be connected to your phone via Bluetooth.

Pendulum Clocks

If your personality tilts more towards classy than contemporary, chances are you will love having a pendulum clock in your home. Of course, these clocks take up quite a bit of space but if you have a large wall that you want to decorate, there is no better way to do so than by adding a nice wooden pendulum clock. It will add its own elegance to the entire room and add an artistic vibe.

Complete Your Wall Decor With Designer Clocks And Other Accents

Who needs expensive wall decor and fancy accents to amp up your space? A classy wall clock can do a lot more to make any wall come alive. We have a fascinating collection of wall clocks and table clocks that will solve all your decor problems.
